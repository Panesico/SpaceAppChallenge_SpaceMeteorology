Solar storm

When he gets really really angry he provokes terrible eruptions and fluctuations that affect many things you love to do like watching cartoons and playing computer games. But, don't worry!! Your other friends, the scientist have an amazing tool that is able to predict those mad days. DSCOVR is out there in the space and plays daily to pick up the right information and alert us when something goes wrong.
